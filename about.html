About

I'm currently a doctoral student in the Institute of Synthetic Microbiology at HHU Duesseldorf. During my PhD I'm studying the diversity and functionality of circadian clocks in cyanobacteria. For his work I use only computational approaches to gain new insights. Besides my work in the lab I'm interested in the data analysis and visualization, and I also enjoy teaching these things to biology undergrades. Outside of work Im a sport enthusiast, who actively exercises various sports but also enjoys watching all sorts of sports, from biathlon to olympics or american football.
